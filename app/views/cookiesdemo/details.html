Cookies on Report a fault

Cookies are small files saved on your phone, tablet or computer when you visit a website.

We use cookies to make the Report a Fault service work and collect information about how you use the service.

Essential cookies keep your information secure while you use the Report a Fault service. We do not need to ask for permission to use them.

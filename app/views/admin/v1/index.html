Welcome to the helpdesk

This tool is for setting up, editing, or disabling a hospital trust from the NHS Report a Fault Service.

You will need the following information (usually provided by email) from the FM Manager of the trust:

  • Name of the Trust
  • The domains to be included in the whitelist for authenticating users
  • The email address(es) for helpdesk or helpdesk operators
  • The phone number (and extension) of the helpdesk for the trust
  • Specifics of how you identify the location of a fault - eg PFI number, room number etc.
  • Email address for your Facilities Manager

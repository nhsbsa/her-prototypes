Report a fault

Report a facility fault at your trust using a smartphone, tablet or computer.

Important: Emergencies and health and safety issues

Do not use this service if:

  • the fault you are reporting is an emergency or health and safety issue
  • the area the fault is in is unavailable or unusable

Call the helpdesk on 7324 2342 or extension 324

What you can report

Use this service to report building maintenance issues only.

Before you start

We will ask you for details including:


  • where the fault is
  • what kind of fault it is
  • how urgent the fault is
  • your contact details

You can also add an optional photo of the fault.

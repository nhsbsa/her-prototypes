Report a fault at your Trust

This service lets you report a fault at your Trust online using a smartphone, tablet or computer. You can also phone the helpdesk to report the fault if you prefer.

Important: Are you reporting a health and safety issue?

Do not use this service if:

  • the fault you are reporting is a health and safety issue
  • the area the fault is in is unavailable or unusable

Please call the helpdesk on 7324 2342 or extension 324

What you can report

This service is for reporting building maintenance issues. These are faults with the physical condition of the building and facilities.

What you cannot report

This service is not for reporting soft facilities management services like cleaning, catering, laundry or similar.

What you need to report a fault

Before you start making your report, you need to know:

  • where the fault is
  • how urgent you think the fault is

You can also add a photo of the fault. This is optional

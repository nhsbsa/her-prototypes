Report a fault at your trust

This website will walk you through the steps to report a fault. It should take about two minutes. Once done, you can opt to receive a copy of the report.

You can do this from a smartphone, tablet or desktop computer. You can include a photo of the problem if reporting by smartphone or tablet.

Important: Is this a health and safety issue?

If this is a health and safety issue, or the area unavailable or unusable;

DO NOT use this online service.

Please call the helpdesk on 7324 2342 or extension 324

This service is an additional option to calling the helpdesk, to make fault reporting more convenient.

This service is for building maintenance issues only e.g. faults with the physical condition of the building and facilities.

This does not cover soft facilities management services e.g. cleaning, catering, laundry etc.

Before you start

You'll need:

  • The location of the fault
  • Your estimate of the urgency
  • Photo of the fault (optional)

Report a fault at your trust

Report a fault at your trust online using a smartphone, tablet or computer. You can also phone the helpdesk to report the fault if you prefer.

What you can report

Use this service to report building maintenance issues and issues with soft facilities management services like cleaning.

What you need to report a fault

Before you start making your report, you need to know:


  • where the fault is
  • how urgent you think the fault is

You can also add a photo of the fault. This is optional.
